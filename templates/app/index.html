Poverty isn't just a lack of income, it's the absence of opportunity, hope, and security. Every day, millions of people around the world struggle to meet their most basic needs—food, shelter, education, and healthcare. At Bridge of Comfort Foundation, we believe that no one should have to live in poverty. That’s why we work tirelessly to provide sustainable solutions—empowering families with the tools, resources, and support they need to build a better future.
